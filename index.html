Object Oriented Programming

This module build your programming knowledge further and moves into the commonly used Object-Oriented paradigm (way of doing) of programming. If you wish to work as a programmer the skills and knowledge build and learned in this module will form the foundation of many development jobs, irrespective of the actual programming language you will use. If you do not go into programming then it will also give you a different way of thinking about solving problems in terms of design. You should discuss with your personal tutor if you may be better matched to the IT degree if you do not intend to do computer programming.
